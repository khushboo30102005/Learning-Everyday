## 1. What is Range?

**Range** is the **simplest measure of dispersion** in statistics.  
It shows how spread out the data is by calculating the difference between the **largest** and **smallest** values.

## 8. Role of Range in Data Science

In Data Science & AI, range is used for:
- Initial data understanding
- Feature scaling awareness
- Detecting extreme values
- Comparing spread between datasets



## 9. Summary

Range gives the **quickest snapshot** of data spread, but lacks depth.  
It is best used as a **starting point**, not a final conclusion.
